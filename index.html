Whats New

  • priority reenrollment is open!

    Our Priority Re-enrollment Period allows currently enrolled families to re-enroll for the following school year at any Stratford campus before we open enrollment to families that have submitted applications. To utilize our online re-enrollment system, login to your Stratford Connect account and click on the "Student Enrollment" tab. Look for the button, "Re-enroll NOW!" and follow the steps to re-enroll. Upon submission of your re-enrollment you will be prompted to make your Non-Refundable Tuition (NRT) payment of $500. The NRT is similar to a deposit, as it holds your child's spot. Furthermore, the NRT applies to the total academic tuition; however, should you decide to withdraw, the NRT will not be refunded to you.
